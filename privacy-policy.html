Privacy Policy for Desert Edit Dashboard

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Overview

Desert Edit Dashboard ("the App") is a private financial management tool that integrates with QuickBooks Online to help manage property rental finances. This app is used exclusively by The Desert Edit business and is not available to the public.

Information We Collect

From QuickBooks Online

When you authorize this app to connect to your QuickBooks Online account, we collect:

  • Financial transactions (expenses, revenue, deposits)
  • Bank account names and balances
  • Vendor and customer information
  • Account categories and classifications

Authentication Data

  • QuickBooks OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • QuickBooks Company ID (Realm ID)
  • Token expiration timestamps

How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks data is used solely to:

  • Display financial metrics in your dashboard
  • Generate reports and visualizations
  • Categorize and organize transactions by property
  • Sync bank account balances

Data Storage

  • QuickBooks tokens are stored securely in Firebase Firestore with encryption
  • Financial data is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud Platform)
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is sold or used for advertising

Data Security

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted
  • Firebase security rules restrict access to authenticated users only
  • Access tokens automatically refresh and expire according to QuickBooks standards

Your Rights

As the sole user of this private app, you can:

  • Disconnect QuickBooks at any time through the Settings page
  • Request deletion of all stored data
  • Revoke app access through your QuickBooks company settings

Data Retention

  • Financial data is retained indefinitely for business record-keeping
  • QuickBooks tokens are retained until you disconnect the integration
  • When you disconnect, tokens are immediately deleted

Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • QuickBooks Online API (Intuit Inc.) - Financial data sync
  • Firebase/Firestore (Google) - Data storage
  • Netlify (Netlify Inc.) - Hosting and serverless functions

Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • QuickBooks: https://www.intuit.com/privacy/
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Netlify: https://www.netlify.com/privacy/
