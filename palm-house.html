Our Philosophy

Design with intention. Hospitality with heart.

Every Palm House property is designed in partnership with The Olive Jar, where Dawn Asher's signature approach brings mood, character, and memorable moments to life.

"Functionality with thoughtful grounding design details, and smart storage that guides, will not only promote keeping an organized environment, but also result in clear headspace and motivation."

We believe in multi-sensory experiences, intentional styling, and hospitality choices that nurture. From the first coffee in the morning to the last sunset of the day, every detail is considered.

This is hospitality as it should be: timeless, authentic, and deeply human.
